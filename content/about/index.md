Engineering better software through smarter testing, stronger automation, and a human-first approach to quality. I work with transparency and a supportive approach, applying pragmatism to every testing decision.

I’m **Paul Littlebury** — Quality Engineer and Tester, who has spent the last 25+ years helping teams ship better software through smarter testing, stronger automation, and a more human approach to quality.

I’m a big believer that quality isn’t something you “add on” at the end — it’s a mindset, a culture, and a way of working that improves everything from delivery speed to user satisfaction.

## What I Do

I work at the intersection of:

* Test Engineering (web, mobile, API, performance)
* DevOps & CI/CD
* Accessibility & Inclusive UX
* Quality Strategy & Coaching
* Conversational AI Testing

I build automation frameworks, embed tests into pipelines, improve reliability, coach teams, and explore systems deeply to uncover issues before users ever see them.

## Current work

I’m currently working as a **Conversational AI Quality Engineer for Acorn Compliance**, where I ensure a compliance-focused chatbot is:

* reliable
* context-aware
* accurate
* accessible
* and enjoyable to interact with

I design and run automation suites (Playwright, Mocha/Chai), monitor API health in real time, assess NLP behaviour, and evaluate tone, inclusiveness, and multi-turn logic.

A particular passion of mine is **neurodivergent accessibility** — ensuring the experience is clear, calm, and supportive for all users.

## How I Work

My approach blends hands-on engineering with collaborative problem-solving:

* Building robust, scalable test frameworks
* Integrating testing seamlessly into CI/CD pipelines
* Coaching teams to take shared ownership of quality
* Encouraging user-centric thinking
* Using exploratory testing to find high-value issues
* Championing accessibility as a first-class quality dimension

I enjoy bringing order to complex systems and helping teams feel more confident in their releases.

## Where I’ve Worked

I’ve worked across healthcare, fintech, telecoms, public services, and high-traffic consumer apps. Highlights include:

* Acorn Compliance – Conversational AI quality and automation
* bet365 – rebuilt mobile UI/API test frameworks for Android and iOS
* CGI – SDET Lead across four Scrum teams on a major Flutter banking app
* Ceta Insurance – test strategies, UI/API automation, load testing, CI pipelines
* Tele2 – quality engineering for a custom AI chatbot
* DocComs, KPMG, Wowcher, TNT and many more across web, mobile, and enterprise systems

Every project strengthened my belief that quality engineering works best when it’s collaborative, proactive, and built into every part of delivery.

## What Drives Me

Ultimately, I’m motivated by making things better —

* better user experience
* better engineering practices
* better communication between teams
* better confidence in every release

Whether it’s building frameworks, shaping strategy, or simply asking the right questions, I love helping teams deliver software that feels reliable, accessible, and genuinely helpful.
