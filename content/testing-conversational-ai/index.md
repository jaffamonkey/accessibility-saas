Your conversational AI app should be helpful, stable, compliant, and consistently on-brand — not unpredictable or fragile. I help teams validate behaviour, fix reasoning gaps, improve tone, and ensure that the chatbot serves *all* users well.

## Services

### Automation & Reliability Testing

I rigorously test your AI system’s reasoning, memory stability, and overall resilience by simulating realistic, multi‑turn conversations. I also identify hallucinations, contradictions, and performance bottlenecks, ensuring the system remains reliable even under heavy load. Every workflow is validated end‑to‑end, and automated checks run continuously alongside your deployments.

### Conversational Behaviour Analysis

I analyse how your chatbot behaves across a wide variety of user journeys, uncovering edge cases and patterns that could lead to confusion or drift. This includes examining long-running dialogue consistency, enforcing guardrails, and ensuring the bot maintains a coherent tone and behaviour throughout conversations.

### AI Accessibility & Neurodivergent UX

I evaluate the clarity, cognitive load, and predictability of your AI’s communication style, ensuring the experience is accessible for neurodivergent users and people who benefit from structured, low‑ambiguity messaging. This includes identifying overwhelming patterns, reducing conversational friction, and improving message structure for inclusivity.

### Personality & Tone Governance

I help define, refine, and maintain a consistent voice for your AI. This includes managing confidence levels, reducing unnecessary apologies, aligning responses with brand tone, and preventing emotional or stylistic drift. The goal is a reliable, human‑friendly personality that remains stable across all interactions.
